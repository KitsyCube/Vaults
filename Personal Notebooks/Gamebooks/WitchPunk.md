A futuristic / cyberpunk dystopian world where the witches have to live in secret, their practices outlawed/shunned, their very essence in opposition to the society. They use their ways to get around the system, trying to keep up and still learn more of their culture's ways.
They have to rise up to the defense of the fallen population, those lost, ostracised and marginalized outcasts in the dark of the changed society.
